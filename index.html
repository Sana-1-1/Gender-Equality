Awareness

Gender equality is not only a fundamental human right but a necessary foundation for a peaceful, prosperous, and sustainable world. Providing women and girls with equal access to education, healthcare, decent work, and representation in political and economic decision-making processes fuels sustainable economies and benefits societies and humanity at large.

Currently, gender inequality manifests in many areas, including violence against women and girls, unpaid care and domestic work, and limited access to education and employment opportunities.

Current Statistics

  • 1. Women make up 39% of the global workforce.
  • 2. 130 million girls are out of school worldwide.
  • 3. 1 in 3 women experience physical or sexual violence.
  • 4. Women spend about three times as many hours as men on unpaid domestic and care work.
  • 5. Women and girls are 10% more likely to go hungry than men, but if female farmers had equal resources, 150 million people could be lifted out of hunger.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is gender equality?

Gender equality is the state in which access to rights or opportunities is unaffected by gender.

How can gender equality be promoted?

One way to promote gender equality is to empower women by identifying and addressing power imbalances and giving them more autonomy over their lives. This can help women make decisions and take actions to maintain their reproductive and sexual health.

What are the benefits of gender equality?

Gender equality can help prevent violence against women, improve the economy, and make communities safer and healthier.
